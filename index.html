About Me

Hello! My name is Dan. I'm a hacker, which means I enjoy acquiring a deep understanding of systems and using them in novel and interesting ways beyond their intended purposes. I also enjoy building new systems and websites from configuirng the servers (or going serverless) to designing intuitive user interfaces.

I strive to use the skills I learn in individual areas and apply them to all of my pursuits. My experience as a developer informs how I look for flaws while security testing and my knowledge of security practices allows me to develop secure systems. Whether I'm performing security assessments or developing, I always aim to create the best possible experience for clients and end users. I believe that security does not have to come at the price of usability.

I feel the most accomplished when I'm able to work on projects that benefit others. That's why I make my projects open source when I'm able and why I volunteer my time to causes I'm passionate about. With everything I work on, I hope to leve it better than I found it.
